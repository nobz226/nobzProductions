Graphic design moodboards and urban culture graphics

The photographs in this gallery were taken using a Sony ZV-E10, full manual mode, in Vancouver BC. I tried capturing the beauty and the grit of Vancouver streets My goal is to catch aesthetic things that we normally walk past without really noticing when rushing around through the cityscape.
