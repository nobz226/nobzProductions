Who is Nobz Productions?

What started it all?

The idea of creating something cool and beautiful with my computer stems from my love of urban culture and all of it's many branches, grafitti, hip-hop, extreme sports, underground bands, nu-metal and all the fashion and aesthetics that come with it. I first fell in love with computers and then discovered a whole new world through skateboarding and music.

I wanted to give so much back to these worlds and 25 years later, I still hold the same love for all of those things. In 2019 I started my digital creative identity by making hip hop beats and today I am dabbling in photography, web development, graphic design and so much more.

Mission

My goal is to provide inspiration for digital creatives and music creators while sharing my own journey through beatmaking, photography and graphic design. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn production, a DJ needing quick track, or just someone who loves urban art, I hope you find something here to help enhance your experience. - Eddie (Nobz)
